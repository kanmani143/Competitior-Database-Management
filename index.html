Project Overview

This project involves an in-depth analysis of bike store sales data. The analysis aims to uncover patterns in sales, customer demographics, and product preferences.

Key Insights

1. Sales are highest in the summer months.

2. Urban areas have a higher demand for mountain bikes.

3. Customers aged 30-40 are the most frequent buyers.
